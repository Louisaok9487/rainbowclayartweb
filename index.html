Frequently Asked Questions

Is the clay safe for young children? +-

Absolutely! The safety of our little artists is our top priority. We exclusively use high-quality, non-toxic, super-soft air-dry clay that is certified safe for children and easy for little hands to work with.

What should my child wear to a class? +-

We encourage creative messes! Please send your child in comfortable clothes that you don't mind getting a little bit of color on. While the clay is water-based and generally doesn't stain, it's always best to wear "art-friendly" attire.

Do we need to bring any supplies? +-

Nope, just bring your imagination! We provide all the clay, tools, and materials needed for each project. Everything is included in the class or party fee.

Can my child take their artwork home? +-

Of course! Every child takes their unique masterpiece home at the end of each class. The air-dry clay will harden on its own in 24-48 hours, becoming a lasting treasure to display proudly.
